about me

I am a Greek-born architect currently living in Vienna. I have worked in the construction industry for more than 15 years. As an architect, I always have samples of construction materials around and early on I realised that with the right composition these inexpensive, not typically used for jewelry materials can make for beautiful pieces one could wear. In particular I work a lot with linoleum , a traditionally flooring material, because I love its touch, its smell and its history! As opposed to vinyl, it is a natural material from line seeds (flax seeds) and it is very eco friendly. I also use raw brass and aluminium as another eco friendly choice. There is so much beautiful waste in construction! I hope you like my work and it inspires you as much as it inspires me!
